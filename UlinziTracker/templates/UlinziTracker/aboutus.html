HOW ULINZITRACKER WORKS

Together for safer communities. UlinziTracker connects residents, officers, and administrators in one platform to ensure quick reporting, effective response, and transparent analytics.

Step 1: Report

Residents submit incidents with text, video, audio, or documents.

Step 2: Confirm

Officers review and confirm incidents, adding response notes for clarity.

Step 3: Resolve

Incidents are marked resolved once officers take action.

Step 4: Analyze

Chiefs and admins access analytics to identify trends and improve safety strategies.
